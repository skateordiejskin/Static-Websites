I’m a freelance writer, author and avid reader with a fascination for the printed word. My idea of the perfect afternoon escape involves a steamy cup of hot chocolate, a comfortable chair and a stack of books and magazines to peruse and savor.

Growing up, I dreamed of seeing my byline in a major publication. Since then, I’ve gone on to contribute to major magazines like Family Circle, Woman's Day, All You and Essence. I’m also the author of a how-to guide on being more assertive. The book can be found on HERE on Amazon!
