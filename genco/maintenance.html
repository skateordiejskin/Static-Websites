Maintenance

Prompt service

Our main goal is to keep your elevators moving. We answer our service calls promptly. We respond quickly and put repeat problems to bed. Our mechanics will pinpoint your problems and make necessary repairs. Our mechanics have an average of Twenty years experience repairing and servicing elevators.

Residential Buildings

Genco Elevator Inc. services residential buildings of all sizes throughout the five boroughs. We deal directly with Owners, Managing agents, Co-op and Condo Board of directors. We would be happy to provide a list of references upon request.

Commercial Buildings

We service different types of commercial buildings both public and private. When the N.Y.C. Dept. of Health and Mental Hygiene needed a new elevator service company fast Genco Elevator Inc. responded and kept their elevators running at a moment's notice. Our contracts include various types of buildings and elevators for entities such as the N.Y.C. Dept. of Sanitation, The Dept. of Citywide Administrative Services and the Metropolitan Detention Center.
