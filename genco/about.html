About Us

Genco Elevator Inc. was formed in May, 2005 with one goal in mind, providing quality service at reasonable rates. Our three owners, Michael Naccara, Michael Mongelli and Richard Cohen are available twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week. We are in constant contact with our mechanics and constantly monitor service calls and problems. At Genco Elevator Inc. there are no buffers, no middle men and, presently, no salesman. When emergencies arise we are available to you at all times. We provide the type of personalized service that only a small private company can offer. We look forward to building a long-term relationship. We would be happy to survey your equipment and provide you with a proposal at any time. We provide quality maintenance, repairs, modernizations and testing. We are a N.Y.C. Licensed Inspection agency and clear all violations and deficiencies.
