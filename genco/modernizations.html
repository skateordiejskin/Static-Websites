Modernizations

Here at Genco Elevator Inc., we understand that a modernization of your elevator is a major undertaking. We will do whatever possible to expedite the process and keep the downtime to a minimum. We will do our best to guide you through the process from start to finish. If need be we will work closely with your consultant or representatives to meet all of your needs.

We perform all types of elevator modernizations. We meet with our customers as often as possible to custom design and engineer an elevator system to meet all of our customer's needs. We fully guarantee all of the equipment that we install. We install only non proprietary equipment with the latest technology that can easily be serviced by all elevator companies. We fully guarantee all of the equipment that we install for as long as we have the service contract.
